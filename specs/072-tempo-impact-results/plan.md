# Implementation Plan: Tempo Impact on Practice and Train Results

**Branch**: `072-tempo-impact-results` | **Worktree**: `../worktrees/072-tempo-impact-results` | **Date**: 2026-04-04 | **Spec**: [spec.md](spec.md)
**Input**: Feature specification from `/specs/072-tempo-impact-results/spec.md`

## Summary

Extend the Practice and Train scoring systems so that tempo difficulty affects the final score. Practice sessions completed at a lower tempo multiplier receive a proportionally reduced score (`rawAccuracy × min(1.0, multiplier)`); Train exercises at higher BPM receive a lower penalty for equal accuracy (via `log2(bpm / 80)` normalisation). Both results overlays are updated to display tempo context (BPM and/or multiplier %) directly beneath the score badge. All changes are additive — existing callers are backward-compatible and stored scores are never recomputed.

## Phase 0: Research Output

See [research.md](research.md). All NEEDS CLARIFICATION items resolved in the clarification session. Key decisions:

- **Practice formula**: `score = clamp(round(rawAccuracy × min(1.0, tempoMultiplier)), 0, 100)`  
- **Train formula**: `bpmFactor = clamp(1 - log2(bpm / 80), 0.5, 2.0); score = clamp(round(100 - penalty × bpmFactor), 0, 100)`  
- **`tempoMultiplier` propagation**: embedded in `PerformanceRecord` at construction time  
- **`bpm` propagation**: embedded in `ExerciseResult` at score time  
- **Sessions index**: score-at-save-time only; no retroactive recomputation
