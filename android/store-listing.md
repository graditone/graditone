## Full Description (≤4000 characters)

```
Graditone is a tablet-native sheet music viewer built for practice and performance. Load MusicXML scores, control tempo, navigate with ease — even without an internet connection.

🎵 KEY FEATURES

• Score display — Render MusicXML scores with clean, precise notation including treble and bass clef, ledger lines, time signatures, key signatures, and bar numbers
• Playback engine — Play back scores using a high-fidelity WASM audio engine; adjust tempo in real time
• Offline-first — All scores and settings are cached on your device. Sheet music you've loaded plays without a network connection
• No account required — Open the app and start practising immediately. No sign-up, no login, no subscription
• Piano keyboard view — Optional on-screen keyboard highlights notes as they play
• Multi-instrument support — Scores with multiple staves and instruments are rendered and played back correctly
• Smooth scrolling — Auto-scroll follows the playhead so you can focus on playing, not scrolling

📱 DESIGNED FOR TABLETS
The layout is optimised for landscape tablets, giving you maximum score real estate. Score navigation and transport controls are placed along the bottom edge for easy reach.

♻️ ALWAYS FREE, ALWAYS OPEN SOURCE
Graditone is fully open source (MIT licence) and will always be free — no ads, no in-app purchases, no premium tier.

🔒 YOUR PRIVACY, RESPECTED
Graditone collects zero personal data. Anonymous crash reports help improve stability, but they cannot identify you.
See the full privacy policy at: https://graditone.com/privacy-policy.html

---

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS: Android 9.0 (Pie) or later
```
